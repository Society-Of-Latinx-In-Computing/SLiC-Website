About Us

SLiC is a student organization whose goal is to create a community of success for Latinx and other underrepresented students by providing academic and professional support.

We will be meeting on 10/7/2021 in the Senate Chamber at the Memorial Union. Future Meetings TBD.

    Mission Statement

  • Increase the participation of underrepresented groups in academia and in industry.
  • Develop technical, professional, and leadership skills aside from what the classroom teaches.
  • Grow a network of students committed to mutual growth.

Activities

These are some of the things that we do and things we plan to do in the future.

Professional Development

We help students build a professional resume, cover letter, Linkedin account, practice the elevator pitch, and provide overall guidance on how to be successful in the tech world.

Community Outreach

Reach out to other schools, specifically high schools, to make students excited and aware of tech majors.



Opportunities

We provide students with many different resources for jobs, internships, scholarships, research, conferences, etc.



class="title"> Speakers

Hear professionals from a wide range of industries and backgrounds talk about tech.


Technical Interview Guidance

We help students get technical interviews and pass them.



Projects

We help students work on different projects and collaborate with others to create a portfolio to showcase to employers and other developers.
